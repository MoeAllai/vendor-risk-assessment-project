# Vendor Scenario

## Mock Company

**Company Name:** CloudCore Analytics  
**Industry:** SaaS / Data Analytics  
**Company Size:** 120 employees  
**Assessment Type:** Pre-onboarding vendor risk assessment  
**Mock Role:** Junior GRC Analyst  

CloudCore Analytics is evaluating a new payroll and HR processing vendor as part of its business operations. Because the vendor will process sensitive employee information, the company must complete a third-party risk assessment before approving the vendor for onboarding.

## Vendor Profile

**Vendor Name:** PayLink HR Solutions  
**Service Type:** Payroll and HR processing platform  
**Vendor Category:** Critical HR / Payroll vendor  
**Business Owner:** HR Department  
**Security Review Owner:** Compliance / Information Security  
**Proposed Use:** Employee payroll processing, tax documentation, salary records, direct deposit, and HR reporting  

## Data Involved

PayLink HR Solutions will process sensitive employee data, including:

- Employee names
- Home addresses
- Personal email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Salary information
- Tax records
- Bank account and direct deposit details
- Employment status
- Benefits-related information

Because the vendor will process financial and personal employee data, the vendor is considered **high inherent risk**.

## Business Need

The HR Department wants to use PayLink HR Solutions to improve payroll accuracy, automate tax reporting, reduce manual HR work, and centralize employee payroll records.

Before onboarding the vendor, CloudCore Analytics must confirm that PayLink HR Solutions has appropriate security, privacy, compliance, incident response, business continuity, and access control practices.

## Assessment Objectives

The purpose of the vendor risk assessment is to determine whether PayLink HR Solutions can protect sensitive employee data and meet CloudCore Analytics’ security and compliance expectations.

The assessment focuses on:

- Data sensitivity
- Access control
- MFA and authentication
- Encryption
- SOC 2 report review
- Security questionnaire responses
- Incident response commitments
- Business continuity and disaster recovery
- Subcontractor risk
- Data retention and deletion
- Contract security requirements
- Remediation tracking

## Inherent Risk Rating

| Risk Factor | Rating | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Data Sensitivity | High | Vendor processes employee financial, tax, and personal information |
| Business Criticality | High | Payroll disruption could affect employee payments and business operations |
| External Access | Medium | Vendor hosts and manages the platform externally |
| Regulatory / Privacy Impact | High | Employee personal and financial information requires strong protection |
| Overall Inherent Risk | High | Sensitive data and critical business process |

## Final Assessment Direction

Based on the mock assessment, PayLink HR Solutions may receive **conditional approval**.

This means the vendor can move forward only if high-priority risks are addressed through remediation, contractual requirements, or documented risk acceptance by management.
