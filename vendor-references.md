# Vendor References

## Purpose

This document lists frameworks, concepts, and simulated evidence connected to the Vendor Risk Assessment Project.

The project is a mock GRC portfolio project and does not use confidential company or vendor data.

---

## Frameworks and Standards Referenced

### SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria

SOC 2 reports provide independent assurance over a service organization’s controls. In this project, the vendor’s SOC 2 Type II report was reviewed to assess whether PayLink HR Solutions has controls related to security and access management.

Relevant areas include:

- Logical access controls
- User provisioning and deprovisioning
- Privileged access review
- MFA and authentication
- Incident response
- Business continuity
- Vendor and subprocessor management
- Monitoring and logging

---

### NIST Cybersecurity Framework

The NIST Cybersecurity Framework helps organizations identify, protect, detect, respond, and recover from cybersecurity risks.

This project connects to:

- Identify: vendor inventory, data classification, and business criticality
- Protect: access control, encryption, MFA, and data handling
- Detect: monitoring and logging
- Respond: incident notification and response coordination
- Recover: business continuity and disaster recovery

---
