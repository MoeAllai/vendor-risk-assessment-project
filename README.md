# Vendor Risk Assessment Project

## Project Overview

This project simulates a Junior GRC Analyst assignment focused on third-party risk management for a mock SaaS company evaluating a new payroll vendor.

The goal is to demonstrate practical GRC skills in vendor due diligence, security questionnaire review, SOC 2 report review, risk rating, remediation tracking, conditional approval, and executive reporting.

## Mock Scenario

CloudCore Analytics is a 120-employee SaaS company evaluating a new payroll and HR processing vendor called PayLink HR Solutions.

Because the vendor will process sensitive employee data, including personal information, salary records, tax documentation, and bank account details, the vendor must complete a security and compliance review before onboarding.

## Project Scope

The assessment covers the following vendor risk areas:

- Vendor intake
- Data sensitivity review
- Inherent risk rating
- Security questionnaire review
- SOC 2 Type II report review
- Access control review
- MFA and authentication review
- Incident response review
- Business continuity review
- Subprocessor risk review
- Data retention and deletion review
- Contract security requirements
- Remediation tracking
- Final approval recommendation

## Vendor Risk Summary

PayLink HR Solutions is classified as a **high inherent risk vendor** because it will process sensitive employee payroll, tax, banking, and personal information.

The vendor appears to have a structured security and compliance program, but several risks require remediation or contract clarification before full approval.

## Key Findings Summary

The assessment identified several vendor risk concerns, including:

- Vague incident notification timeline
- Unclear data deletion timeline
- Incomplete subprocessor review evidence
- MFA exception tracking weakness
- Incomplete access review evidence
- Privileged access approval gap
- Outdated business continuity test evidence
- Vendor-managed encryption keys only
- Cyber insurance evidence not provided
- Bridge letter may be needed

## Final Recommendation

The recommended decision is:

**Conditional Approval Pending Remediation**

This means PayLink HR Solutions may continue through onboarding, but high-priority risks must be addressed before production use or contract signature.

The most important remediation items are incident notification language, data deletion requirements, subprocessor oversight, MFA exception tracking, access review evidence, privileged access approval evidence, and current BCP/DR testing.

## Analyst Conclusion

This project demonstrates how a Junior GRC Analyst can support third-party risk management by reviewing vendor evidence, validating questionnaire responses, analyzing SOC 2 report notes, identifying risks, documenting remediation actions, and communicating a final vendor recommendation to leadership.
